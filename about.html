Our Story

Founded in 2018 by MD Samim, Esamtech Studios began with a simple mission: to create utility applications that solve real-world problems without unnecessary complexity. What started as a solo endeavor has rapidly grown into a leading player in the mobile tech industry.

Today, our focus extends beyond simple utilities. We specialize in designing intuitive user interfaces and architecting robust, scalable cloud backend systems that can handle heavy lifting—ensuring that whether you are hosting images, tracking networks, or streaming media, the experience is lightning fast and flawlessly reliable.
