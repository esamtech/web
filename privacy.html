Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Your privacy is important to Esamtech Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information across all our mobile applications and services.

Since our inception in 2018, we have prioritized user privacy and data protection. We build utility apps, browsers, and media tools designed to function primarily on your device, collecting only the absolute minimum data required to provide a seamless experience.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on which of our applications you use, we may collect or request access to certain types of information. We only access this data when it is directly relevant to the app's core functionality:

  • Device & Usage Data: We may collect generic, non-identifying information such as device models, operating system versions, app crash reports, and general usage metrics to help us improve app stability.
  • Network Data: For apps requiring internet access (e.g., browsers, IPTV players, IP trackers), standard network data such as IP addresses may be processed momentarily to fulfill your direct requests.
  • Local Storage & Media: Apps that generate, download, or edit files (e.g., image hosts, video downloaders) will request access to your device's local storage. We do not transmit your personal files to our servers unless explicitly initiated by you (such as uploading to a cloud feature).
  • Camera & Location: Specific utilities (like GeoCam) may request camera and GPS/location permissions to overlay accurate tags. This data is processed locally on your device and is not tracked or stored by Esamtech Studios.

2. How We Use Your Information

The minimal data we do collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our applications.
  • To identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues.
  • To respond to your customer service inquiries and support requests.
  • To display localized non-personalized or personalized advertisements (where applicable and consented).

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications may utilize third-party services to function effectively. These services have their own independent privacy policies. We commonly use:

  • Google Play Services: For core Android functionality and app distribution.
  • Analytics Providers: Tools like Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app stability and crash reports without identifying individual users.
  • Advertising Networks: We may use platforms like Google AdMob to display ads. These providers may use cookies or advertising IDs to serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad tracking preferences within your device's system settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is never sold to data brokers. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations, valid subpoenas, or court orders.
  • To protect and defend the rights or property of Esamtech Studios.
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with our services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard secure protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our applications are generally intended for a broad audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take necessary actions to remove that information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
